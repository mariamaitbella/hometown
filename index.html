Visit Taroudant

There is enough to see and do in the city for a day or two, but the best thing about Taroudant is its location in the fertile Souss valley and at the foothills.

Ride horse cart

Taroudant Caleche Carriage Tours are very wonderful tours around the historical city, the amazing walls, between souks, frequently the normal & simple Roudani People inside & outside the city. Tours duration is 30 min to 1 hour .

Visit claudio Bravo palace

The Claudio Bravo Palace is a magical place, still bathed in the presence of Claudio Bravo, the great hyperrealist painter who built it. Since 1972, the artist, of Chilean origin, had made Morocco his place of life and creativity.

Artisana shopping

Taroudant craftsmanship is a renowned activity, specializing in the manufacture of leather, wrought iron, wood carving, jewelry, carpets and ironwork.
